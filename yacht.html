HOW WE DO IT

THE BIBBO PROCESS

A methodical approach built for marine environments — no shortcuts, ever.

01
Inspection

We assess the full vessel — hull condition, oxidation level, teak, interior, and engine room before any work begins.

02
Rinse & Pre-Wash

High-pressure fresh water rinse to remove loose salt, debris, and surface contamination from all surfaces.

03
Decontamination

Marine-specific degreasers and cleansers break down salt deposits, staining, and biological growth on the hull and deck.

04
Polish & Protect

Machine polishing restores gloss, followed by your chosen protection — marine wax, sealant, or ceramic coating for lasting defense.
